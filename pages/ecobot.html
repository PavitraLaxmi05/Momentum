How can I reduce my household energy consumption?

Great question! Here are some effective ways to reduce your household energy consumption:

  • Switch to LED light bulbs, which use up to 75% less energy than incandescent bulbs
  • Unplug electronics when not in use or use smart power strips to eliminate phantom energy use
  • Improve insulation in your home to reduce heating and cooling costs
  • Use a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling
  • Wash clothes in cold water and hang dry when possible
  • Upgrade to energy-efficient appliances with ENERGY STAR ratings
  • Use ceiling fans to reduce air conditioning needs
